Basmati Rice Recipe

This recipe makes the most perfect basmati rice every time, and it’s so simple! Light, fluffy, and aromatic.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Basmati rice
  • 1 1/2 cup water
  • 1 TBSP of olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 cloves
  • 3 cardamom seeds
  • 1/4 stick of cinnamon

    • Directions

    1. Pour the rice and water together in a medium sized pot.
    2. Swish them both around using your hands to rinse the grains, and then carefully drain the cloudy water.Keep doing this until the water is nearly clear.
    3. With the rinsed rice already in the pot, pour 1 ½ cups of water over top along with the oil and salt.
    4. Bring it to a boil (uncovered), cover with a lid, and turn the heat down low.
    5. Let the rice simmer until all of the water has been absorbed.
    6. Remove the pot from the heat and let the rice steam for at least 5 or 10 minutes.
    7. Fluff the finished rice with a fork and serve!
